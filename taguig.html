Taguig

Coordinates: 14.5167° N, 121.0500° E

Description

Taguig is a highly urbanized city in Metro Manila, Philippines. It is known for the Bonifacio Global City (BGC), one of the country's leading financial and lifestyle districts. Historically a fishing village, Taguig has experienced rapid growth and modernization in recent decades.

Key Information

  • Country: Philippines
  • Region: National Capital Region (NCR)
  • Population: Approximately 886,722 (2020 census)
  • Landmarks: Bonifacio High Street, The Mind Museum, Venice Grand Canal Mall, American Cemetery and Memorial
  • Economy: Finance, real estate, retail, information technology
