Welcome to Taguig City

The perfect blend of urban progress and provincial charm.

The "Probinsyudad" of Metro Manila

Taguig has transformed from a humble fishing village along the shores of Laguna de Bay into one of the Philippines' most important financial and lifestyle districts. Home to the bustling Bonifacio Global City (BGC), it's a hub of culture, innovation, and opportunity, while still retaining its rich history and heritage.

Discover the Sights

Bonifacio Global City

The premier financial district known for its modern skyscrapers, vibrant street art, world-class shopping, and dynamic nightlife.

Venice Grand Canal Mall

Experience a taste of Italy with a romantic gondola ride along the man-made canal in this uniquely themed lifestyle mall.

The Mind Museum

A world-class science museum with over 250 interactive exhibits that make learning about science fun for all ages.

American Cemetery & Memorial

A serene and solemn memorial with beautifully manicured lawns, honoring the soldiers who lost their lives in WWII.

Market! Market!

A bustling shopping complex where you can find everything from trendy fashion and electronics to regional delicacies.

St. Anne Parish Church

A historic church that stands as a testament to Taguig's deep-rooted faith and cultural heritage, dating back centuries.
